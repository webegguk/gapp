Source / AMP Add-on at Washington Post

This is a good way of introducing Google AMP into existing and/or larger sites that require an SEO boost. The uncertainty of Google AMPs uptake means that moving over to it entirely is not a good idea.

The Guardian and Washington Post are two good examples of how this technique has been implemented.

They both have two alternatives of their articles. The main site has two tags which, when AMP is installed, will serve up the relevant version to the user on mobile via Googles Javascript!
